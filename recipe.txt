Place a non-stick frying pan onto the stove
Spread a light layer of butter onto the frying pan
Set a plate near the stove and place a tortilla on it
Spread cheese and other addons onto the tortilla
Place another tortilla ontop of the first tortilla
Turn the stove to low
Place the uncooked quesadilla onto the stove gently, ensure that ingredients inside the tortillas do not fall out
Every minute, flip the quesadilla until the tortillas are of desired level of cooked
Turn off stove, and serve!
